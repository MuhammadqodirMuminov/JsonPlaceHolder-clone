Routes

JSONPlaceholder comes with a set of 6 common resources:

  1. /posts GET, POST, DELETE, PUT
  2. /users GET, POST, DELETE, PUT
  3. /comments GET
  4. /todos GET, POST, DELETE, PUT
  5. /albums GET

Filters

JSONPlaceholder has more filters of 6 common resources:

  1. /posts filter with ?userId=1
  2. /users filter with ?username=Bret
  3. /todos filter with ?userId=1
